Recommended Budget Plan

Basic Needs Budget

  • Food: 40% - Home-cooked meals using affordable ingredients.
  • Transportation: 30% - Public transit, walking, or carpooling.
  • School Supplies: 10% - Essentials like pens, notebooks, and printing.
  • Others: 20% - Utilities, emergency savings, or minimal leisure.

Balanced Living Budget

  • Food: 35% - Healthy meals with occasional dining out.
  • Transportation: 25% - Public transit, biking, or fuel expenses for private vehicles.
  • School Supplies: 15% - Covers necessary materials and optional resources.
  • Others: 25% - Leisure, savings, utilities, or social activities.

Flexible Comfort Budget

  • Food: 30% - Quality meals, regular dining out, or specialty groceries.
  • Transportation: 20% - Ride-sharing, private vehicles, or convenience options.
  • School Supplies: 20% - Advanced tools, software, or special projects.
  • Others: 30% - Entertainment, travel, investments, or luxury expenses.
